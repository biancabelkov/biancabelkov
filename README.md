## 👋 Hi, I'm Bianca

I'm on the talent team at **Commence**. We build modular, data-driven systems that transform how federal and commercial healthcare programs operate. Our platform focuses on data transformation and interoperability, turning complex, messy healthcare data into something people can actually use to make decisions. Think fraud detection, quality improvement, and claims analytics built in production, not in a slide deck.

We're a small but growing team. You won't be a ticket in a queue here. The problems are real, the data is messy, and the work shows up in programs that directly impact the people who depend on them.
